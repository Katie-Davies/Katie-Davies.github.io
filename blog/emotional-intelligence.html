EQ vs IQ

What is EQ?

The ability to understand, process and effectively express your feelings. Also being able to engage with the feelings of others.

There are five pillars:

  • Self awareness- recognising your own emotions, understanding how they influence your actions
  • Self regulation- managing your emotions in a healthy way.
  • Empathy- understanding others emotions and offering comfort and support
  • Social skills- effective communication and building and maintaining relationships
  • Intrinsic motivation
Some characteristics of EQ: Actively listening and providing comfort Breaking down tasks to alleviate stress Setting goals Emotional intelligence will help improve all areas of your life, help you be a better partner, leader, parent, friend. It allows you to mediate conflict, regulate your anxiety and reduce stress.
